# About Genki

**Genki** is an independent publication launched in March 2022 by [Thian](https://twitter.com/thianisoneos/). If you subscribe today, you will get full access to the website, as well as email newsletters about new content as it becomes available. Your subscription makes this site possible, and allows Genki to continue to exist. Thank you.
